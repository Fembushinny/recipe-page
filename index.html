Boiled Yam and Egg Sauce

Key ingredients: Egg, tomatoes, peppers, and onions

Description

This is a simple and delicious recipe for boiled yam and egg sauce. It is a popular dish enjoyed for breakfast, lunch, or dinner.

Ingredients

  • Salt: 1 teaspoon (to taste)
  • 2-3 medium tomatoes (chopped)
  • 1 large onion (chopped)
  • 1-2 scotch bonnet peppers (chopped)
  • Seasoning: 1 bouillon cube, 1 tsp curry powder, 1 tsp thyme, and salt to taste

Instructions

  1. Boil yam until tender, then drain.
  2. Sauté onions, tomatoes, and peppers.
  3. Mix eggs with vegetables and seasoning.
  4. Cook until eggs are set.
  5. Serve and enjoy your meal!

Nutritional Facts

This dish provides carbohydrates from yam, protein from eggs, and essential vitamins from vegetables.

Variation Tip

For extra flavor, you can add diced bell peppers, garlic, ginger, or even leafy vegetables like spinach or fluted pumpkin (ugu) to the sauce.

Time

Preparation Time: 10 minutes

Cooking Time: 60 minutes

Total Time: 70 minutes
